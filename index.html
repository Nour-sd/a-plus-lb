About A Plus lb

A Plus lb is an educational platform built for students and teachers in Lebanon. It gives learners a clean place to discover courses, request support, communicate with approved teachers, submit work, and unlock paid course content after payment confirmation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality education easier to reach, more organized, and more personal. We support students after school, during exam preparation, and while building practical skills that can help them in real academic and career paths.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted Lebanese learning community where students can grow with confidence and teachers can share legal, respectful, professional, and useful educational content.

What we provide

Services made for real student needs

A Plus lb provides online tutoring, daily agenda support after school, school and university course help, English and Arabic language learning, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint and Excel training, web development courses, SAT preparation, exam revision, and support for student projects. Students can choose a course, apply, chat with the teacher, arrange payment through the approved number, and unlock the course using a private code after admin confirmation.
